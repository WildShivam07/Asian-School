Asian school has been established by a registered body called "Babu Lal Sah and Ram Khelawan Sah Trust."The trust believes that the progress of a nation solely depends upon the progress of her children.The school is located at Brahampura in Muzaffarpur,Bihar.The trust has alloted about 5 acres of land for the school to provide much needed educational facilities to the people of Bihar.
Established in 2006 and affiliated to CBSE Delhi it is a Co-educational English Medium School run on public school lines.
Through curricular and co-curricular activities , learning is made a joyful experience . keeping in mind the overall development of the child .Asian school gives equal emphasis on theory and practical .The school follows all international Teaching Methodology which ensures that education is not restricted to text books learning only but extends right up to its practical application.A unique approach is applied using modern teaching Aids like computer , CD , and projectors to make the students learning process stress-free , enjoyable and above all meaningful.

Asian School,a co-educational institute is a beautiful synthesis of ancient values and a futuristic approach.It is an amalgam of Indian tradition,contemporary views,a harmonious blend of the east and the west.The kids who study here will surely be proud of our Asian School in owning it his/her own alma mater.
Asian School took its birth on 26th March 2006.As its logo i.e.,"Because every child deserves the best"says,our Asian School has stood magnificiently in Muzaffarpur,the second largest city in Bihar.our school provides such a facility that can evolve a child not as a good human being but also global citizen.
